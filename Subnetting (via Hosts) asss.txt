Subnetting (via Hosts)

1. Design a network for teleperformance.com with 360 administrators, team leads, and managers.
Use the IP address space 10.0.0.0/8

Convert (bit length): 9
Subtract: /32 - 9 = /23 (3rd, 2i)
Ipasok: si 2i sa 3rd octet = 10.0.2.0

teleperformance.com: (NETWORK): 10.0.2.0  /23
Valid Range:
 - First Valid IP:  		10.0.2.1 /23
 - LAST Valid IP: 		10.0.3.254
Last IP(BROADCAST): 		10.0.3.255

NOT Network:			10.0.4.0 /23
